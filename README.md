# Ferns & Petals Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from Ferns & Petals (FNP), a gifting and e-commerce company, to understand customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, product performance, and delivery efficiency. The dashboard was developed using Microsoft Excel to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights.

## Objective

The objective of this project was to identify key revenue drivers, customer spending patterns, seasonal sales trends, and top-performing products to support data-driven business decisions.

## Dashboard Insights

### Revenue by Occasion

* Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan generated the highest revenue.
* Holi contributed significantly to overall sales performance.
* Seasonal occasions played a major role in revenue growth.

### Revenue by Category

* Colors emerged as the highest revenue-generating category.
* Soft Toys and Sweets were among the top-performing product categories.
* Product category analysis helped identify customer preferences.

### Monthly Revenue Trends

* August recorded the highest revenue.
* February and November showed strong sales performance.
* Sales patterns revealed seasonal demand fluctuations.

### Customer Spending Analysis

* Average customer spending exceeded ₹3,500 per order.
* Indicates strong customer willingness to purchase premium gifting products.

### Geographic Analysis

* Imphal, Dhanbad, and Kaval recorded the highest order volumes.
* Regional demand insights can support targeted marketing campaigns.
